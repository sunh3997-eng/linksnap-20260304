LinkSnap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary

LinkSnap is a Chrome extension that saves webpages with AI summaries. We have no backend server and collect zero user data.

What We Collect

Nothing. LinkSnap does not collect, transmit, or store any information on external servers.

What Stays on Your Device

  • API Keys — stored in chrome.storage.local, never transmitted to LinkSnap
  • Saved Links & Summaries — stored in your browser's IndexedDB, local only
  • Settings — stored in chrome.storage.local

Third-Party API Calls

When you save a page, LinkSnap sends the page content directly from your browser to the AI provider you configured (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq). This request goes from your browser to their servers — LinkSnap is not involved. Please review the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — read the current page's URL and title when you click Save
  • scripting — extract page text to send to the AI for summarization
  • storage — save your settings and API key locally
  • host_permissions (api.openai.com, etc.) — make API calls directly to AI providers
